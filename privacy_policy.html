Privacy Policy

iNature • Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Information We Process

iNature processes images you choose to scan, local app settings, and local scan history data. By default, iNature does not require account registration.

2. How We Use Information

Image data is used to run on-device wildlife identification and safety analysis. Scan history is stored locally so you can review previous results.

3. On-Device Processing

Core recognition runs on your device. Data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share, export, or back up content using system features.

4. Permissions

Camera permission is used for photo capture. Photo library permission is used for importing photos. You can change permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Local scan records remain on your device until deleted by you. You can clear history from the Settings page in the app.

6. Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. iNature does not receive your full payment card data.

7. Children

iNature is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data inappropriately, contact us and we will assist with review and deletion guidance.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is shown in the app and identified by the updated date above.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact arxivchat@gmail.com.
