We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: Maintaining and improving Application features, processing your requests

  • Medical Support: Providing medical knowledge, diagnostic suggestions, and health management tools
  • Communication: Sending updates, notifications, marketing information, and customer support
  • Analytics and Research: Conducting data analysis to improve the Application and user experience
  • Security and Compliance: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security issues

We do not sell your personal information or medical data to third parties.

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, or processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from those in your country.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information and medical data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Using SSL/TLS encryption to protect data transmission

However, please note that no security system is completely impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information.
