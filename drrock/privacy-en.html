Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

DrRock is an application for identifying stones, minerals, and rocks. This Privacy Policy explains how we process relevant information when you use features such as photo recognition, album-based recognition, and record storage.

By using DrRock, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

2. Information We May Collect

  • Image Information: Images of stones, minerals, or rocks that you capture or select from your photo library for recognition, analysis, and result display.
  • Recognition Records: Recognition results, saved items, notes, history records, and other information you choose to store in the app.
  • Device and Log Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, network status, error logs, and basic usage data used to maintain app operation and troubleshoot issues.
  • Feedback Information: Questions, suggestions, contact details, or related screenshots that you voluntarily submit.
  • Payment Information: If the app offers subscriptions or value-added services, payments are processed by the app store or third-party payment platforms, and we do not store complete bank card information.

3. Permissions We Use

  • Camera Permission: Used to capture images of stones or minerals for recognition.
  • Photo Library Permission: Used to select existing images for recognition or save related results.
  • Network Permission: Used to submit recognition requests, load recognition results, sync subscription status, or obtain necessary services.
  • Notification Permission: Used only with your authorization if reminder or result notification features are provided.

You can manage these permissions in your system settings. Disabling permissions may prevent photo recognition, album recognition, or online recognition features from functioning properly.

4. How We Use Information

  • To identify stone, mineral, or rock categories and display related descriptions.
  • To save, display, and manage your recognition history, favorites, and notes.
  • To maintain app stability, security, and basic service capabilities.
  • To process feedback, troubleshoot issues, verify subscriptions, and provide customer support.
  • To improve recognition quality and product experience after de-identification or anonymization where applicable.

5. Images and Recognition Data

To complete recognition, the images you submit may be processed on your local device or on servers. We process these images and results only to the extent necessary to provide recognition, save records, improve services, or comply with legal requirements.

Please avoid uploading images that contain identification documents, faces, addresses, license plates, or other unrelated sensitive personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

DrRock may use third-party services for image recognition, cloud storage, crash analysis, analytics, push notifications, payment processing, or subscription verification. We only share the minimum necessary information with third parties to enable the relevant functions.

We do not sell your personal information.

7. Storage and Security

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy and take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect data security. Because the internet environment is not absolutely secure, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information during transmission or storage.

8. Your Rights

You can delete recognition records, manage favorites and notes within the app, and disable permissions such as camera, photo library, and notifications through system settings. To the extent permitted by applicable law, you may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information related to you.

9. Minors

Minors should use DrRock with the consent of a parent or legal guardian and under their guidance. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from minors.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Policy based on feature changes or legal requirements. After an update, the new version will be published on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our handling of personal information, please contact us through the in-app feedback channel or the developer's publicly available contact details.
